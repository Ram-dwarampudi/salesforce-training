What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.
It is a technology or software used by companies .to interact with all the departments like sales, services, managment, ect, in a single paltform.

Why Do Companies Use Salesforce?

1)Store customer data securely in the cloud
2)Automate business processes
3)Improve sales management
4)Track customer interactions

Important Salesforce Concepts

1) Account
An Account represents a company, organization, or customer with whom a business has a relationship.opuurtunity 

2) Contact
A Contact represents a person associated with an Account.

3) Opportunity
An Opportunity represents a potential business deal or sales process.

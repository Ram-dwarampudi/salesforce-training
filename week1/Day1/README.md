
What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. It is a technology or software used by companies .to interact with all the departments like sales, services, managment, ect, in a single paltform.

Why Do Companies Use Salesforce?

1)Store customer data securely in the cloud 2)Automate business processes 3)Improve sales management 4)Track customer interactions

Important Salesforce Concepts

Account An Account represents a company, organization, or customer with whom a business has a relationship.opuurtunity

Contact A Contact represents a person associated with an Account.

Opportunity An Opportunity represents a potential business deal or sales process.

Real world mapping for college admissions

Accounts == college

contact == parent /student

oppurtunity == admission process
